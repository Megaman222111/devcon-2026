# Chapter 3.8 — Ending a Shift

**Module 3: Basic Security Procedures**

---

## Section 1: Preparing for Handover

When your shift is ending:

1. **Review your notes** for information to pass to the incoming guard
2. Share details about:
   - **Incidents** that occurred during your shift
   - **Premises information** (e.g., burned-out light not yet replaced)
   - **Special events** occurring (late-night gathering, expected contractor)
   - **Anticipated personnel arrivals**

> Some of this information may not be in the post orders — equipment breakdowns or last-minute changes may not be updated before the new shift arrives.

---

## Section 2: Before Leaving

- Check that your **replacement is ready** for work and at their post
- Confirm they have **no further questions** for you
- Advise them **when you are leaving** the post
