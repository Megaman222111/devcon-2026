# Module One: Introduction to the Security Industry

When you decided to enroll in this course, you presumably did so because you have a desire or need to work in the security industry in Alberta. You may already be familiar with the roles of security professionals, or you may be new to the field altogether. Whether you have worked in the field in the past, the present, or not at all, you can benefit from the information provided in this module.

Learning Outcomes
